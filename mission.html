Foundation of Vision

XELTIS founded by Sapiens Ndatabaye to tackle mineral mismanagement. Began developing Nova7, the AI-powered mineral intelligence platform.

First Breakthroughs

Completed Shabunda (gold) and Walikale (tin) missions in DRC, saving $450K and supporting 5,000 people with jobs and infrastructure.

Scaling Impact

Katanga (cobalt, lithium) and Katana (copper) missions uncovered $41.5K in minerals, saved $550K, and empowers 5,000 locals.

Global Reach

Maniema (rare earths) and South Kivu (coltan) missions generated $465K, supported 7,000 people, and mapped 80+ countries.

Pioneering the Future with AI

XELTIS redefines mineral intelligence with AI-driven precision, achieving 98% accuracy in mapping resources across 80+ countries. Our Nova7 platform processes satellite data in real-time, uncovering deposits with unparalleled efficiency. In 2022, we mapped $465K in rare earths in Maniema, saving $550K in costs. This innovation drives sustainable mining, reshaping global resource management.

Mining for a Greener Tomorrow

Sustainability is at XELTIS’s core. We champion eco-friendly mining, minimizing environmental impact while maximizing efficiency. Our 2020 DRC project empowered 5,000 locals with sustainable jobs and infrastructure, saving $450K through optimized processes. Using AI and green strategies, we ensure mineral extraction benefits both planet and people, forging a balanced future.

Building Global Bridges

Collaboration fuels XELTIS’s mission, uniting 71 global partners to advance mineral intelligence. In 2022, our South Kivu project with international allies supported 7,000 people and mapped resources across 80+ countries. By fostering trust and transparency, we create networks that empower communities and industries, equitably sharing cosmic resources.
